A technical exhibition is an event where businesses showcase their latest technological advancements. The exhibition provides a platform for companies to promote their products and services, network with other businesses, and learn about industry trends. It plays an important role in the business world by providing a platform for companies to showcase their innovations.

 

GIGABIT

A coding event is a gathering of coders, developers, programmers, and tech enthusiasts to collaborate, network, and showcase their skills. These events come in various formats, including hackathons, coding challenges, code sprints, or coding conferences. Coding events play an important role in the tech industry by fostering innovation, collaboration, and community among coders and developers.

 

KURUKSHETRA

A gaming event is a gathering of video game enthusiasts and industry professionals to showcase, play, and learn about the latest gaming technologies and products. These events offer a platform for playing Valorant and BattleGrounds Mobile India(BGMI). Gaming events play an important role in the gaming industry by promoting new technologies, fostering community, and driving the growth of the industry.

 

ROBOSYNC

Robotics events are gatherings of industry professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts to showcase, learn about, and discuss the latest advancements in robotics technologies. These events feature exhibits, demonstrations, and talks on topics ranging from industrial robotics to artificial intelligence. Robotics events play an important role in the industry by promoting innovation, collaboration, and community among robotics enthusiasts and professionals.

 

STRATEGEM

A business idea pitch event is a gathering of entrepreneurs and investors to pitch and discuss new business ideas. These events offer a platform for entrepreneurs to showcase their innovative ideas and gain funding, mentorship, and networking opportunities. Pitch events play an important role in the startup ecosystem by promoting entrepreneurship, innovation, and economic growth.
